    Brand history
    2020
    To better take the corporate responsibility of environmental protection, EBICO has registered the EBICO(CHINA) Environment Co., Ltd for the further promotion of innovation of low-nitrogen, energy saving and environmental protection attributes of EBICO burners in China. Also, concentrating on “Internet+” thinking, EBICO has organized its online and offline sales team and tried to achieve the balance of both of them so as to respond to “the Belt and Road” policy, thus striving to earn the international market.
    2019
    With the Asian headquarter in Wuxi, EBICO has deployed more than 20 marketing channels or service outlets all over China, such as Shanghai, Jiangsu, Shandong, Guangdong, Xinjiang, the northeast region, the southwest region, the northwest region and so on. That is also why EBICO has kept its leading role in thermal industry of China.
    2013
    In 2013, EBICO reorganized itself into EBICO S.r.l Company and the established an independent R & D Department, specializing in the burner and its system development and improvement work, focusing on the thermal field of cutting-edge technology. Decades of experience, In the global thermal industry, EBICO has gradually grown into a technology-based company with a strong competitiveness in integrating brands, technologies and services.
    1998
    In 1998, EBICO entered the Asian market and began the R & D and manufacturing of standard burners and non-standard burners. China is one of the fastest growing markets in the world.
    1965
    Since 1965, the company has been engaged in the design and production of Die Casting, packaging and standard industrial burners.
