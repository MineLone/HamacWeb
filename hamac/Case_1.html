8-25 Tons Boiler Burner Low Nitrogen Transformation Project for Beijing Benz Automotive Co.

8-25 Tons Boiler Burner Low Nitrogen Transformation Project for Beijing Benz Automotive Co.
Cooperation Partner：
Beijing Benz Automotive Co., Ltd.
Industry：
Cases for Heating Industry
Application Products：
ES-G Series of Low-nitrogen Burners

Beijing Benz Automotive Co., Ltd. (abbreviated as Beijing Benz) was established on August 8, 2005. It is a Sino-German joint venture that is invested by Beijing Automotive Co., Ltd., Daimler AG and Daimler Greater China Investment Co., Ltd., and integrating R&D, engine and vehicle production, sales and after-sales service.

 

As the representative of the capital auto industry's continuous transformation and upgrading towards high precision, Beijing Benz relies on the spirit of craftsmen and implements "digitalization, flexibility, and greenness" in every aspect of production. Low nitrogen remodel like a breeze of spring. EBICO hit it off with Beijing Benz, and Provide customized solutions for low-nitrogen reconstruction for two 8-ton boilers.

 

After on-site inspection by the technical personnel of EBICO, the ES300GE low-nitrogen burner was finally selected. This kind of burner uses low-nitrogen technologies such as multi-stage fuel ratioing and center separation, to reduce NOx emissions data greatly, which perfectly fits with the value proposition of Beijing Benz's "Green Demonstration Factory" .

After the renovation project is completed, the measured emission concentration of nitrogen oxide is 26mg/m3, which is realized the original intention and moved towards the goal of "Beijing Intelligent Manufacturing Benchmark Enterprise" steadily.
