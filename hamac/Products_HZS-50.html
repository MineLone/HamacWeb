Asphalt Mixing Plant Burner
As a kind of mechatronics equipment with high degree of automation, the asphalt mixing burner has a combustion flame heat transfer method for direct contact radiation, short flame shape control but negative pressure design of the combustion chamber. Product advantages include low pressure oil supply, medium atomization; low pressure gas, gas ring atomization; low NOx emissions.
