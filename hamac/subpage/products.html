Boiler Burner

The burners for boilers are important equipment for burning fuel oil, gas, coal powder and other particles or for fully burning various fuels after alternation and mixing. EBICO can provide burners suitable for various fuels, including Integrated machine, split machine, non-standard customization machine. The emission conforms to the regulations of China, the United States and the European Union, especially for NOx emissions.

Learn More
Thermal Oil Furnace Burner

The heat-conduction oil furnace is a kind of equipment that uses the indirect heating of heat-transfer oil by burning the burner, forces the heat-transfer oil after the liquid-phase circulating heating through the circulation pump, transmits the heat energy to the heat equipment. EBICO burners are available in temperatures up to 200° C, 250 ° C or even 500 ° C and can achieve low NOX combustion.

Learn More
Asphalt Mixing Plant Burner

As a kind of mechatronics equipment with high degree of automation, the asphalt mixing burner has a combustion flame heat transfer method for direct contact radiation, short flame shape control but negative pressure design of the combustion chamber. Product advantages include low pressure oil supply, medium atomization; low pressure gas, gas ring atomization; low NOx emissions.

Learn More
Hot Blast Furnace Burner

The hot blast stove directly or indirectly transfers the heat of pulverized coal to the heating equipment such as drying equipment, and is divided into two modes: circulation heat and non-circulation heat. The EBICO burner can provide burner products that meet the requirements of negative pressure, positive pressure and high temperature for the characteristics and requirements of the furnace.

Learn More
Non-Standard Burner

"EBICO" ET series non-standard custom burners are designed and produced according to the special needs of customers. According to the special fuel, special combustion mode, special working condition, special structure, etc, the special design team of EBICO can realize the customization of burner.

Burner Management System

The EBICO burners need to be equipped with certain system products, including fuel delivery systems, air supply systems, and intelligent control systems. The concept of the integrated control system is to improve the control precision and save the control cost; The fuel delivery system can be further divided into a liquid fuel delivery system and a gas fuel delivery system.
